Community Pregnancy Centers

The Community Pregnancy Center is located in Middletown, Ohio. CPC is a Christ-centered organization offering pregnancy tests for those who might be expecting; a 15-week Mom2 Be program teaching prenatal care & nutrition, prenatal development, newborn care & more; Ultrasounds; a Dad2Be program to assist young men as they prepare for fatherhood; and compassionate care & support for post-abortive mothers & fathers; and a Care Closet supplying diapers, formula, clothing, and other necessities at no cost.



For more info on CPC, please visit their website at www.pregnancychoice.net
